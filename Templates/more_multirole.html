Multi Role System

The CarShare platform offers a multi-role system, ensuring that every type of user can interact with the platform based on their needs. Whether you're a car owner, a renter, or an administrator, each user role is designed to provide the appropriate features and access.

Car owners can list their vehicles, set rental prices, and manage bookings through the platform. Renters can browse through the available cars, select the one that suits their needs, and book it for the desired period. Administrators oversee the entire platform, ensuring smooth operations and providing user support when needed.

This multi-role approach ensures a seamless experience for everyone involved, enhancing the overall efficiency of the platform.
